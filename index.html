Fusion Fighters






So, What is a Fusion Fighter?

Fusion Fighters are high-tech androids created by NexTech that people can use to battle from all over the world. In the old days, people put together pieces of scrap metal to create robots, but with modern technology, the sport has evolved into something that anyone (with enough money) can participate in. People all over the world use Fusion Fighters to battle for sport, for money, or both! As someone new to the art of Fusion Fighting, you will need to create your own Fusion Fighter by getting one from NexTech and customizing to your heart's content!


Customizing?

Yes! You can customize your Fusion Fighters. In fact, that the entire purpose! Unlike traditional fighting games, Fusion Fighters is focused around the concept of creating fighters by customizing your appearance, moves, and more. You will unlock more items to customize with over time, allowing for more and more combinations and interactions! Modify your character's outfit, their moves, ultimates, and even special badges that grant passive or active bonuses. Mix and match, and find your favorite combination and use it to beat your opponents!


But Where Will We Fight?

I'm glad you asked! There are a variety of places you can fight your rivals, ranging from cluttered alleyways riddled with hazards to grand arenas, owned by yours truly, filled with spectacle and amazement! Beware, as certain stages have tricks up their sleeve that you can either turn off, or leave on and use to your advantage to mix things up!

Meet H1-R0 and LU-C

H1-R0 and LU-C are the newest model of Fusion Fighters created by NexTech. These models are the most customizable yet, allowing their owners to customize their moves, badges, outfits, and way more.


Outfits

Customizing your Fusion Fighter's outfits will modify their stats. This will change a number of factors from their speed to their weight, which will impact how they play.


Moves

Customizing your Fusion Fighter's moves will give them different actions they can perform. You can bind any move to any direction on the L-stick plus either A or B on the gamepad. Moves bound to A will be chargeable, which moves bound to B will be fusable... Whatever that means...


Badges

You can give your Fusion Fighter up to 3 unique badges which grant passive and active abilities. For example, the Burst Badge allows you to perform a burst by using up some of your energy meter, and the Hover Badge allows you to slowly descend by holding the jump button in mid-air.


Ultimates

There are a variety of Ultimates to choose from for your fighter. Each one can only be used once your energy meter is fully charged, but has a massive effect. Some will heal you, some will grant buffs, and some just deal damage. Create your build around your Ultimate for the best strategies!

Engaging Stages!

Fight with your friends on a variety of stages. Each one with its own special perks that make it unique. These are toggleable, of course, in case you and a buddy just want the vibe of the stage without the complications.


Custom Stages

Just like you can create custom fighters, you can also create custom stages! You can use the tile editor to lay out a variety of tiles to create the stage layout of your dreams! But that's not all...


Gizmos

Gizmos are special interactable things you can place in stages, these range from teleporters to bounce pads to item boxes and much, much more. Create whatever stage your heart desires!

Pre-made Rulesets

A variety of pre-made rulesets exist, such as:

Competitive

Battle it out without worrying about items or stage gizmos getting in your way!

Casual

Have some good fun with your friends and use an item or two to your advantage...

Sport Mode

Compete with others to get the highest score in this extreme sport!

Coin Mode

Collecting coins will boost your energy meter. Last one standing wins!


Custom Rulesets

Not only can you create custom fighters and stages, but custom rulesets as well! With custom rulesets, you can decide which stages are selectable, which items spawn, how the winner is decided, and much more!
